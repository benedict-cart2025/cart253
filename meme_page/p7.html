AUGUST 2019

20/08/19

I had a weird and not so great thing happen to me today at work. For one, the higher-ups have asked me to make it so that our data will be given to law enforcement agencies so that their job can be easier, to track suspects and crimnials of the like. However with the problem of police brutality, racial or social prejudice and a whole lot of other issues, I think that the data we give them might make things worse.

The way how law enforcement may undergo their operations whether on a local and national level may be greatly dangerous and reckless if the data is used in a partial and unjust manner. I all of this, just doesn't feel right anymore, I wanna stop doing this soon...
